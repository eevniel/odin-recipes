Eggs and Bacon Breakfast Tacos

Home

Sure, breakfast tacos make sense for breakfast, but if you're a fan of having breakfast for dinner like me, then why not enjoy them for your last meal of the day? These breakfast tacos are incredibly easy to throw together when you need dinner in a pinch.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 4 slices of bacon, cooked
  • 4 tortillas
  • taco cheese
  • salsa
  • butter (for cooking)

Preparation

Step 1

Scramble up the eggs in a bowl.

Step 2

Heat up a skillet with a small sliver of butter. Once the butter is completely melted, pour the eggs onto the hot skillet.

Step 3

Scramble until the eggs are completely cooked through.

Step 4

Separate the eggs into four tortillas. Crumble the bacon and sprinkle on, along with the taco cheese and salsa.

Step 5

Serve immediately.
